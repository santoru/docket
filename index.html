Docket

A beautiful, minimal task manager that lives in your macOS menu bar. Integrates with Apple Reminders — iCloud, Siri, and Apple Watch support out of the box.

macOS 14+ Swift & SwiftUI Zero Dependencies Liquid Glass MIT License

Everything you need, nothing you don't

🔄

Reminders Sync

Two-way sync with Apple Reminders. Your tasks on iPhone, Apple Watch, and Siri — automatically.

📅

Smart Dates

Type "tomorrow 3pm" or "next friday" — natural language parsed into due dates instantly.

⌨️

Global Hotkey

⌘⇧D opens Docket from anywhere. Double-press to quick-add a task without leaving your flow.

🏷️

Labels & Lists

Color-coded labels, multiple lists, and smart filtering. Organize your way.

🔁

Recurring Tasks

Daily, weekly, or monthly — tasks auto-recreate on completion. Never forget the routine.

🎨

Themes & Glass

9 pastel themes, custom colors, and native Liquid Glass on macOS 26. Make it yours.

👆

Swipe Gestures

Swipe right to complete, left to delete. Confetti on every win. Undo if you change your mind.

🔔

Smart Reminders

Get notified before tasks are due. Configurable from 5 minutes to 1 day ahead.

⚡

Zero Dependencies

Single build.sh script. No Xcode project, no SPM, no CocoaPods. Pure Swift compiled with swiftc.

Get started in seconds

./build.sh && open build/Docket.app

Requires macOS 14+ and Xcode Command Line Tools
