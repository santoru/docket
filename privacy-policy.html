Privacy Policy

Docket does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your tasks stay on your device.

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Data Collection

Docket collects no data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reporting
  • No user accounts or registration
  • No network requests to external servers
  • No advertising or tracking

Data Storage

All your tasks and preferences are stored locally on your Mac in your user directory. If you enable Reminders Sync, data is shared with Apple Reminders via the system EventKit framework — this is governed by Apple's own privacy policy and your iCloud settings.

Third-Party Services

Docket does not integrate with any third-party services. The optional Reminders sync uses Apple's built-in frameworks and communicates only with Apple's servers under your iCloud account.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, reach out via GitHub Issues.
